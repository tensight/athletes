Walter "Sneeze" Achiu is a Chinese-American, Honolulu, Hawaii native who is a pioneer in breaking the color barrier of American sports. In 1927, he became [the first player of east Asian descent to play in any professional American sports league](https://americanfootballdatabase.fandom.com/wiki/Walter_Achiu).

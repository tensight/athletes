Yuta Watanabe is a professional basketball player for the NBA. He was born in the Kagawa Prefecture of Japan, and in 2014, became the [first Japanese-born basketball athlete to receive a Division I scholarship to play basketball](https://gwsports.com/sports/mens-basketball/roster/yuta-watanabe/6153) and fourth Japan-born player ever to play in Division I in the United States.

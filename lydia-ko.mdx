Lydia Bo-Gyung Ko is a New Zealand Golfer who regularly competes on LPGA tours. On February 2, 2015, she became [the youngest golfer ever of either gender to reach the number 1 ranking in professional golf](https://www.rolexrankings.com/rankings/2015-02-02).
